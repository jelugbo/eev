Find Your Study Buddy

Working with other students offline can help you get the most out of an HEBS course and even increase the likelihood you will successfully complete the course. So, your homework is to find a study buddy.

The HEBS Meetup Community is a fantastic way to find study buddies in your area. Meetups make it easy for students to organize get-togethers or study sessions in coffee shops and cafes, libraries, or at local schools. If there isn’t a HEBS Meetup Community in your area, you can easily start one up.

The course specific discussion forums are also a great place to find neighbors or even new friends to invite to a Meetup you are looking to organize or even a virtual Google Hangout. Make sure you also join the HEBS community on Facebook, Google+ or Vkontakte and to share with friends your HEBS experience. You may even inspire a friend to join HEBS and enroll in the course with you.
